About AFCSOA

The Automated Fare Collection System Operators Association (AFCSOA) is a dedicated platform for uniting transit authorities, agencies, and stakeholders across the globe. Established in 2017, AFCSOA seeks to drive the future of public transit through collaboration, standardization, and innovation in automated fare collection systems.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a network that facilitates sharing of knowledge, expertise, and resources among the global automated fare collection community, ensuring sustainable and progressive development in public transit ecosystems.

Vision

Guiding transit stakeholders towards a future where seamless, efficient, and innovative automated fare collection systems are ubiquitously adopted, enhancing the transit experience for all.

Values

  • Collaboration: Building a network of shared knowledge and cooperation.
  • Innovation: Encouraging advancements in AFC technologies.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency and ethics in all operations.
  • Excellence: Striving for superior quality in standards and practices.
