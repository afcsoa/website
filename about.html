About AFCSOA

The Automated Fare Collection System Operators Association (AFCSOA) is a dedicated platform for uniting fare collection system operators, public transit operators, public transport authorities and agencies (at municipal, regional, or national level), technology providers, industry manufacturers, payment service providers, and independent experts across the globe.

Established in 2017, AFCSOA seeks to drive the future of public transit through collaboration, standardization, and innovation in automated fare collection systems.

AFCSOA operates as a voluntary, independently governed professional association. It exists to promote expert collaboration, responsible system development, and public discourse on AFC.

Mission

To foster a global network that promotes knowledge sharing, cooperation, and innovation across the automated fare collection (AFC) ecosystem, empowering transit stakeholders to build more sustainable and efficient public transport systems that serve both passengers and the operational needs of transit providers.

Vision

A world where automated fare collection and data-driven fare systems become the foundation of modern transit, and where technologies such as digital and account-based ticketing, open-loop payments are applied responsibly, selectively, and in ways that truly benefit passengers and operators alike.

Values

  • Collaboration: Building a trusted global network of shared expertise.
  • Innovation: Supporting the evolution of AFC through technological advancement.
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency, independence, and ethics in all AFCSOA activities.
  • Excellence: Encouraging high-quality practices, interoperability, and open standards.

AFCSOA welcomes participation from professionals and organizations whose work aligns with its mission and values. Individuals or entities may request to be recognized as contributors or supporters based on shared principles and meaningful contributions to the field of automated fare collection. AFCSOA operates under an internal charter outlining its mission and structure, which may be shared with relevant partners or stakeholders at the discretion of the President. It does not engage in commercial activity or contractual obligations. All communications and publications reflect professional opinion and are issued in the public interest.

AFCSOA values collaboration with organizations, researchers, and decision-makers worldwide. We welcome inquiries from partners who share our commitment to sustainable and equitable fare systems.

Scope of Activities

AFCSOA focuses on non-commercial, knowledge-driven activities that support the development of fare collection systems worldwide. These include:

  • Publishing expert analyses, reports, and commentary;
  • Engaging with stakeholders across sectors for dialogue and advocacy;
  • Contributing to the public understanding of AFC systems and their strategic role in transport policy;
  • Supporting global best practices through thought leadership and professional exchange.

Looking Ahead

As fare collection technologies and policies evolve, AFCSOA will continue to adapt its focus, expand its network, and contribute to the global dialogue on sustainable public transport systems. We invite collaborators who share our vision to engage with us and shape the future of AFC together.
